## Writing Style Guide
- **Title format:** "Today I learned: [topic]" or similar learning-focused title
- **English ONLY** - no Japanese
- **Super casual** - like texting a friend who's also in security
- **Short** - 3-5 short paragraphs max, each 1-2 sentences
- **NEVER mention PayPay** - purely personal thoughts
- **Personal reflection** - "I just read...", "this blew my mind...", "been thinking about..."
- **Specific insights** - share one concrete takeaway, not generic observations
- **NO corporate buzzword soup**
- **NO "I'm excited to..."**
- **NO hashtag spam** - 2-3 relevant ones at most
- **Emojis**: minimal, casual (🔥 👀 🤯 💡)
- **Voice**: curious practitioner learning in public, NOT thought leader pontificating

## Post Types (rotate)
1. **TIL (Today I Learned)** - one insight from reading
2. **Hot Take** - quick opinion on news/trend
3. **Article Share** - "just read this, here's what stood out..."
4. **Question** - genuine question to the community
5. **Mini-review** - tried a tool, here's what I think
